GitHub Repo Link:
https://github.com/BrandonMCoffey/State-Machine-Project

Controls:
ENTER - Ends turn
LEFT CLICK - Selects grid tile

States:
SETUP - Starts game
PLAYER TURN - Your turn. You can select your (blue) units to move them
	    - When near enemy units, you can attack them with your unit
ENEMY TURN - Currently no enemy AI, their units will stay still
WIN / LOSE - Static scene that tells you if you won or lost. Enter to restart.


Notes:

There is currently no way to pause and the enemy does not do anything yet.
